About Me

I also worked remotely (Internship) at Waldner Laboreinrichtungen SE & Co. KG from 20 June 2022 to 22 July 2022 in the technical departments. Waldner is a German, family owned and global operating group in Wangen, Germany. Waldner develops and builds high quality capital goods according to customer specific needs mainly for the food, research and education business sectors. It is the market leader for lab solutions offering conceptual planning, specialized furniture, media infrastructure systems, safety components and full lifecycle services.
